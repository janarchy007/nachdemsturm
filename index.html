Working Line German Shephards

We are a small, professional operation which started out from a life-long love of the working-style German Shepherd breed. Each of our dogs are very much a part of our family and live with us inside our home. As puppies they are exposed to normal daily activities and are well accustomed to the comings and goings of neighbors and friends. In our breeding program, we strive to produce and preserve the quality and traits of what we believe the working German Shepherd should have. We only select for our breeding program dogs that have been health tested, come from good bloodlines, and would not only be suitable for professional work, but would also make wonderful family companions.




Who We Are...

The Start of Something Great
In Lake Jackson, Texas

Nach dem Sturm German Shepherds is a breeder of top working line German Shepherd Dogs in the Brazoria County area. Our breeding goals focus on drives, health, and temperament while maintaining breed standards. We strive to provide families with healthy pets that are able to perform as the breed was intended.
Our puppies make great family companions as well as being fit to compete in IGP/IPO/Schutzhund, PSA, American Schutzhund, Agility, AKC Obedience, Rally, Dock Diving, Nosework, or train for SAR.
