E Ozias Kep Aus Der Asche


Sire


Oz is a stunning, large sable male with strong lines and a masculine presentation. He has very good conformation with a strong head and good expression. He is a confident, attentive dog with a balanced nature and strong nerves, and works eagerly for either the ball or food. He also enjoys spending time with his people as he has a nice "off switch". As part of our training program Oz has been started in Search and Rescue - Area Search. Ozias's sire, Pepper von Kap Karthago, is an IGP Grand Champion. We believe his pedigree has a lot to offer in our breeding program.

Nyx Kep Aus Der Asche


Dam


Nyx is a beautiful bi-colored female with a plush coat, nice head and feminine expression. She is a very loving and high energy girl with a pleasant disposition, strong nerves, and intense focus. As part of our training program, Nyx has been started in Search and Rescue and shows a propensity for Area Search and HRD. She brings a real powerhouse pedigree to our program, preserving some of the old lines.
